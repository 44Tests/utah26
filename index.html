About the Pre-ECNL: The Pre-ECNL is ECNL's developmental program for U10–U12 players, serving 300+ clubs and 800+ teams across the country. Cup winners earn automatic bids to the Champions Cup, which also includes at-large invitations for additional top-performing teams. The Northern Cal Cup concluded March 13–15, 2026 — full flight results for that event are pending official ECNL recap publication.
